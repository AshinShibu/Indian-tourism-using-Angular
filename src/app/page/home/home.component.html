About

India is a vibrant and diverse country renowned for its rich cultural heritage and stunning landscapes. Key attractions include the iconic Taj Mahalin Agra, the tranquil backwaters of Kerala, and the golden deserts of Rajasthan. Nature lovers are drawn to the scenic hill stations of Shimla and Manali,while spiritual seekers visit Varanasi and Rishikesh. Adventurers explore the valleys of Ladakh, and relaxation seekers flock to the beautiful beaches of Goa and the Andaman Islands, making India a global tourism hub.
